Neer Dosa

Preparation Time: 5 mins
Cooking Time: 12 mins
Total Time: 17 mins
Servings: 4 (10 Dosas)
Ingredients
  • 1/3 cup raw rice, soaked for 4 hours and drained
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for greasing
Method
  1. To make Neer Dosa, combine the drained raw rice and 1/2 cup of water in a mixer and blend till smooth.
  2. Transfer the mixture into a deep bowl, add 1 to 1/2 cups of water and salt and mix well.
  3. Heat a non-stick tawa (griddle) till red hot, sprinkle a little water on the tawa (griddle) and wipe it off gently using a muslin cloth.
  4. Grease the tawa (griddle) with a little oil and smear it by using a cut onion.
  5. Pour approx. 1/3 cup (approx. 5 tbsp) of the batter on it and tilt the tawa (griddle) in a circular motion to make a round dosa.
  6. Cover with a lid and cook on a medium flame for 1 minute. Fold the dosa into a triangle.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter to make 9 more dosas.
  8. Serve Neer Dosa immediately with chutney.
